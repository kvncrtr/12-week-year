# 12-week-year
In 3 years, I have a strong Christian family culture, a healthy marriage, children growing in structure and biblical principles, household income of $250k–$500k+, no consumer debt, strong savings and investments, senior-level backend engineering capability, and either a high-income software role or profitable software business.

# Yearly

- **Establish weekly intentional time with your wife**
    - One protected date night or intentional block per week.
    - No job search, coding, or business work during that time.
    
    Steps
    
    1. Establish a time
    2. place it on the calendar
    3. decide the type
        - **At-home connection**
            - Cook together.
            - Watch something together.
            - Dessert and conversation.
            - Board game or card game.
            - Sit outside and talk.
        - **Out-of-home date**
            - Dinner.
            - Coffee shop.
            - Walk at a park.
            - Bookstore.
            - Museum.
            - Dessert spot.
        - **Faith-centered block**
            - Pray together.
            - Read a Psalm or Proverb.
            - Talk about what God is teaching each of you.
            - Discuss family values.
        - **Planning-light block**
            - Talk about the week.
            - Review stress points.
            - Align on family schedule.
            - Discuss one financial or household topic.
            - Then end with something enjoyable.
    4. create a menu
        - Dinner out
        - Coffee and walk
        - Dessert date
        - Movie night at home
        - Cook a new meal together
        - Bible study and prayer
        - Walk around a nice area
        - No-phone conversation night
        - Vision night
        - Game night
        - Picnic
        - Try a new restaurant
        - Sit in the car and talk with coffee
        - Go to Target or a bookstore together
        - Listen to music and talk
    5. Ask Koren something simple:
        
        > “I want us to have one protected block each week where I am not coding, job searching, or working. What would make that feel meaningful to you?”
        > 
    6. decide a failed block so if Thursday nights don’t work then we move it to Saturday morning
    7. ask any of these 
        
        “How are you really doing this week?”
        
        “What is one thing you appreciated this week?”
        
        “What felt heavy this week?”
        
        “How can I support you better this week?”
        
        “What would make you feel more loved right now?”
        
        Pray together for your marriage, family, children, work, and future.
        
    8. show I love you 
        - Pick the place.
        - Bring her favorite snack.
        - Write a short note.
        - Plan the babysitting/logistics.
        - Choose an activity she likes.
        - Compliment her specifically.
        - Ask about something she cares about.
        - Pray for her out loud.
        - Send a message earlier in the day saying you are looking forward to the time.
        - Handle a task beforehand so she can relax.
    9. simple cadence for the week 
        1. **Sunday**: schedule the time and confirm with her
        2. **day before**: choose from menu
        3. **day off**: choose a pursuit, shut down everything no distractions during this time.

- **Save 3-6 months of expenses**
    - Q1 — Control + Starter Safety
        - Get full visibility of finances (debt, spending, budget)
        - Stop financial leakage (no new debt)
        - Identify consistent monthly surplus
        - Build starter emergency fund ($1K–$2K)
        - Outcome: You are in control and stable
    - Q2 — Debt Reduction + Systems
        - Aggressively pay down 25–50% of debt
        - Maintain strict budget discipline
        - Start small automatic savings
        - Outcome: Cash flow improves and systems begin
    - Q3 — Debt-Free + Wealth Foundation
        - Eliminate all consumer debt
        - Fully automate saving
        - Build 1–3 months of expenses saved
        - Increase savings rate significantly
        - Outcome: You shift from surviving to building
    - Q4 — Financial Security + Expansion
        - Reach 3–6 months emergency fund
        - Run fully automated financial system
        - Generate consistent surplus for investing
        - Outcome: You are financially secure and ready to build wealth
